Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 loaves of bread
  • butter
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 2 slices of ham
  • lettuce

Preparation

  1. spread butter over the 2 slices of bread
  2. add one slice of cheese
  3. add one slice of ham
  4. add the lettuce
  5. add another slice of hame
  6. add another slice of cheese
  7. put the other leaf on top of the other one
  8. Enjoy!
